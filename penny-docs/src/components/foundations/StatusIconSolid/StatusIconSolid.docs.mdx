## Usage

- Use in the **header of a modal** to immediately communicate the outcome or nature of the content.
- Use **`success`** for modals confirming a completed action, like "Payment sent" or "Vendor added".
- Use **`warning`** or **`issues`** for modals reporting an error or a problem requiring attention, like "Payment failed" or "Bank account disconnected".
- Use **`informative`** or **`help`** for standard confirmation dialogues or modals that require user input, like "Confirm payment details".

## Variants

### Status variant

The `variant` prop sets the icon's glyph and color to a semantic meaning. Warning and pending share the warning color; informative and scheduled share the informative color; processing, help, and issues use the primary (neutral) color; alert, decline, and cancel use the critical color.
